CHASE ATLANTIC

Chase Atlantic (occasionally stylized as CHASE ATLANTIC) is an Australian R&B band , Queensland, formed in 2014. The group consists of three members: Christian Anthony, Clinton Cave and Mitchel Cave. Chase Atlantic has released seven EPs and three albums.
